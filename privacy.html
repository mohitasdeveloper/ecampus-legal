Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 16, 2026

Welcome to ECampus (“the App”), owned and operated by Mohit Mali (“we”, “our”, or “us”). ECampus is a private social networking platform designed for verified college students. At present, the platform is intended primarily for students of B.K. Birla Night College. By using ECampus, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we may collect the following information.

Account Information

When you register, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Student email address or Student ID
  • Password (stored securely using authentication services)
  • Phone number
  • Course or academic information
  • Profile photograph (optional)
  • Biography and profile details (optional)

Phone numbers are collected solely for identity and student verification purposes.

User Generated Content

Information you voluntarily create includes:

  • Posts
  • Images
  • Stories (Hotposts)
  • Comments
  • Likes and reactions
  • Polls
  • Profile information
  • Campus event posts
  • Messages or reports submitted through the App

Content that you choose to share may be visible to other authorized users depending on your privacy settings.

Device Information

When you use ECampus, we may collect limited technical information such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • IP address
  • Log information necessary for security and troubleshooting

We do not collect precise location information.

2. Permissions We Request

ECampus may request the following permissions.

Camera

Used only when you choose to:

  • Upload a profile photo
  • Create a post
  • Create a Hotpost (Story)

Photos and Media

Required to allow you to select existing images from your device.

Notifications

Used to send:

  • Connection requests
  • Likes
  • Comments
  • Replies
  • Campus announcements
  • Important account notifications

You can disable notifications through your device settings.

Internet Access

Required for authentication, syncing content, loading posts, and accessing campus services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify student identity
  • Display your profile
  • Deliver social networking features
  • Provide campus updates
  • Improve app performance
  • Protect users from abuse
  • Detect fraud and unauthorized activity
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

ECampus uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies where applicable to:

  • Keep you signed in
  • Remember preferences
  • Improve performance
  • Maintain secure sessions

These technologies do not track your activity outside ECampus.

5. Sharing of Information

We only share information with trusted service providers necessary to operate ECampus.

Supabase

Used for:

  • User authentication
  • Secure database hosting
  • Data storage

Cloudinary

Used for:

  • Image uploads
  • Image optimization
  • Media delivery

These providers process data only as necessary to provide their services. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted network communications (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication
  • Access controls
  • Authentication tokens
  • Database security measures

No internet-based service can guarantee absolute security, but we continually work to protect your information.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Maintain your account
  • Provide our services
  • Meet legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our policies

Inactive or deleted accounts may have information removed according to our operational requirements.

8. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account at any time. When your deletion request is processed, we permanently delete:

  • Account information
  • Profile
  • Posts
  • Stories
  • Comments
  • Likes
  • Uploaded media
  • Associated personal information

Some information may be retained where required by applicable law or for legitimate security purposes.

9. Children’s Privacy

ECampus is intended only for verified college students. Users must be at least 16 years old to use the service. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below the permitted age. If we become aware that information has been collected from an ineligible user, we will remove it promptly.

10. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

  • Keeping passwords secure
  • Providing accurate information
  • Respecting other users
  • Following Community Guidelines
  • Using ECampus lawfully

Users are responsible for the content they publish.

11. International Data Processing

Your information may be processed using secure cloud infrastructure operated by our service providers. By using ECampus, you consent to the processing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

12. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request to:

  • Access your information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Contact us regarding privacy concerns. Requests may be submitted to: ecampus@mohitmali.in

13. Third-Party Links

ECampus may contain links to third-party websites or social media profiles. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party services.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When significant changes are made, we will update the Effective Date and, where appropriate, notify users through the App. Continued use of ECampus after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

15. Contact Us

If you have any questions, requests, or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact:
