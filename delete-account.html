Account & Data Deletion

As per Google Play's Data Safety policies, you have the right to request the complete deletion of your ECampus account and all associated data (including posts, images, and profile information).

How to request deletion:

  1. Send an email to your-email@mohitmali.in from the email address associated with your ECampus account.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include your Full Name and Student ID in the email body.

Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete your authentication record, database profile, uploaded media, and all social interactions within 7 business days. This action cannot be undone.
